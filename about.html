About CPS&C Malir

Building Tomorrow's Leaders with Knowledge, Passion, and Integrity

Our Legacy

Cantt Public School & College (CPS&C), established in 1987 under the Ministry of Defence, Government of Pakistan, is one of the leading educational institutions in the Malir Cantonment region of Karachi. Over the years, CPS&C has evolved into an esteemed academic institution that excels in delivering top-notch education while focusing on character-building and leadership development.


With a rich history spanning over three decades, we have consistently maintained our position as a center of academic excellence and character development. Our institution has produced numerous distinguished alumni who have gone on to achieve remarkable success in various fields, including medicine, engineering, business, and public service.


We aim to create an environment where learning is not just about acquiring knowledge, but also about understanding the importance of responsibility, ethical values, and community. CPS&C prepares students for the challenges of the modern world with a balanced approach that integrates academics, sports, arts, and cultural activities.

Our Campus

Our state-of-the-art campus spans over 25 acres of meticulously maintained grounds, featuring:

  • Smart Classrooms equipped with interactive whiteboards, high-speed internet, and advanced multimedia systems
  • Science Laboratories with modern equipment for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Computer Labs with the latest hardware and software
  • Olympic-sized swimming pool and comprehensive sports complex
  • Digital Library housing over 50,000 books and online resources
  • 500-seat auditorium with professional sound and lighting systems
  • Modern health clinic staffed with qualified medical professionals
  • Extensive playing fields for cricket, football, and athletics

Our Values

  • Integrity: We foster honesty, ethical behavior, and moral courage in all aspects of student life
  • Excellence: We pursue the highest standards in academics, sports, and character development
  • Respect: We celebrate diversity and promote mutual understanding among our multicultural community
  • Innovation: We encourage creative thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Community: We build strong relationships between students, faculty, and families
  • Compassion: We nurture empathy and social responsibility
