Educational Trips

At Cantt Public School & College Malir, we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom. Our students regularly participate in study trips to various educational and cultural sites, enriching their knowledge through real-world experiences. These trips are carefully selected to complement the curriculum, offering students a deeper understanding of subjects while also fostering teamwork and social interaction.

Apart from academics, we focus on overall student development through extracurricular activities, sports, and events. Students are encouraged to explore their talents and passions, building confidence and developing leadership skills that will serve them in all aspects of life.
