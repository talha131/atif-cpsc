Introduction

Introduction to Cantt Public School & College, Malir Cantt – Karachi Region


Established in 1987 under the auspices of the Cantonment Board Malir, Ministry of Defence, Government of Pakistan, Cantt Public School & College (CPS&C) has been a cornerstone of quality education in the Karachi region. Since its inception, CPS&C has remained steadfast in its commitment to fostering academic excellence, character development, and leadership skills among its students.


Recognized today as one of Karachi's premier educational institutions, CPS&C features a purpose-built, state-of-the-art campus equipped with modern amenities. These include well-equipped science and computer laboratories, a comprehensive library, spacious playgrounds for sports and physical activities, and a multi-purpose auditorium for events, assemblies, and cultural programs. These facilities create a vibrant environment that nurtures creativity, innovation, and holistic growth.


At CPS&C, emphasis is placed on a balanced education, blending academics with extracurricular and co-curricular activities to ensure the all-rounded development of students. With a proud legacy of excellence, CPS&C continues to inspire and shape future leaders, standing as a beacon of quality education in the Karachi region.

Vision

Dedicated to a continuing ML&C tradition of excellence in an ever-changing world. Within a safe and congenial environment, to provide relevant, high-quality education and prepare a dynamic student body for future endeavors at par with global standards by instilling in them critical thinking skills, and respect for core values of honesty, loyalty, perseverance, and compassion. To honor achievements and promote pride in ourselves, in our school, and in our community

Mission

The mission of CPES is to provide a comprehensive learning environment, integrating various modules of STEAM, thus expanding learning horizons to new dimensions where cantonment residents are valued and respected. All staff members in partnership with parents and families are fully committed to the academic well-being of learners, empowering them with cognitive and work readiness skills and becoming socially responsible citizens.
